Innovation, 3D Modelling

Thermogrip

Thermogrip

I developed the ThermoGrip, an innovative wireless mouse powered by body heat, to address the environmental concerns of battery waste and the inconvenience of constantly recharging wireless mice. Inspired by thermoelectric technology, the ThermoGrip transforms hand warmth into electricity, offering a sustainable, battery-free experience. Its design features a Peltier module and a lightweight heatsink to efficiently generate power from the user's palm heat. This unique approach not only reduces electronic waste but also enhances user convenience by providing a truly wireless experience without the need for external power sources. The ThermoGrip stands out for its sustainable technology and ergonomic design, marking a significant step towards eco-friendly computing accessories.

Innovation, 3D Modelling

Aqua Nova

Aqua Nova

Aqua Nova is a pioneering and sustainable approach to tackle the global challenge of water scarcity, especially for sea nomads and remote communities. My innovation stands out with its self-cleaning solar desalination system, uniquely designed to convert seawater into safe drinking water efficiently and affordably. Utilizing natural sunlight, the system employs a novel wick structure for effective salt removal and a semi-spherical aluminum plate for accelerated evaporation, ensuring purity and safety.

My approach is environmentally conscious, using recycled plastic materials from the oceans for the pod's construction, which not only addresses the issue of plastic pollution but also promotes community involvement in ocean clean-up efforts. The modular design of Aqua Nova enhances operational safety and flexibility, allowing for various platform shapes and sizes to suit different community needs. Distinct from traditional, costly, and energy-intensive desalination methods, Aqua Nova offers an eco-friendly, low-cost alternative, harnessing solar energy and promoting environmental awareness. This innovation represents a significant step towards a sustainable future, providing clean water and fostering positive social change.
